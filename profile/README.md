## Total Cost Analysis for Clearer Lending Choices

Mortgage Coach total cost analysis is the central idea behind the platform. It helps show how interest rate, loan term, closing cost, mortgage insurance, and cash-to-close choices affect a borrower over time. That makes Mortgage Coach useful when a client is comparing fixed-rate options, evaluating discount points, or deciding whether a refinance makes financial sense.

Mortgage Coach app workflows can also help loan officers explain value beyond the lowest rate. A borrower may see why one offer has a lower payment but a higher long-term cost, or why another option requires more cash now but better aligns with future plans. Mortgage Coach TCA presentations turn these tradeoffs into a visual advisory experience.
